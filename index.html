Learn. Enjoy. Explore.

PRASTUTI is the annual techno-management fest of Electrical Engineering Department, IIT BHU, Varanasi. Synonymous with technical know-how and management acumen, Prastuti is about practical application of classroom knowledge. It’s not just these neck-to-neck competitions that you can get at PRASTUTI. It’s also about the whacky novel contraptions created here that go far beyond the fest. Internships. Patents. All happens. PRASTUTI sets the stage free for you. The inception of Prastuti dates back to year 2001, when it was started as an All India Students' Paper Presentation contest organized by Electrical Engineering Society (EES). The idea of Prastuti as an event was brought up into realization by Prof. S.N. Mahendra of Electrical Engineering Department, and since then there was no looking back.
